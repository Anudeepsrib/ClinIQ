# Policy Lifecycle And Emergency Exceptions

This synthetic policy is for ClinIQ evaluation only.

## Standard Lifecycle

Policy owners must review active hospital policies at least every 24 months. The policy office records document owner, department, effective date, review date, and version.

## Emergency Exceptions

An emergency interim policy exception may be approved by the department director and the policy office. The approval record must include written rationale, risk owner, impacted department, and an expiration date no later than 30 days after approval.

The policy office must review the emergency exception within five business days and either retire it, revise it, or convert it into the standard policy review workflow.

## Administrative Access

Only users with administrator access may create users, delete policy documents, or view department-wide document stats.
