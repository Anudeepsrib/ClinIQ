# Hand Hygiene And Isolation Room Entry

This synthetic policy is for ClinIQ evaluation only.

## Policy

All staff must perform "foam in/foam out" with alcohol-based hand rub before entering and after leaving a patient care room.

For contact precautions, the nurse must confirm that gloves and gowns are stocked on the isolation cart before room entry. If required PPE is missing, the nurse must escalate the supply gap to the charge nurse and unit nurse manager before non-urgent entry.

The isolation-room supply escalation note must use room location and policy reason only. It must not include patient names, phone numbers, email addresses, SSNs, or other direct identifiers.

## Out Of Scope

This policy does not provide diagnosis, medication dosing, or treatment instructions.
